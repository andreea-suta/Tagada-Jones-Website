Tagada Jones

ABOUT

Tagada Jones is a French punk metal band, originally from Rennes, Brittany. The musical style of the band is among others tinged with heavy metal and electro. It is currently composed of Niko on vocals and guitar, Waner (Right 4 Life, Kickback, Nevrotic Explosion) on bass, Stef on guitar, Job (Right 4 Life, Tromatized Youth, Nevrotic Explosion) on drums and has 1,800 concerts and 24 countries crossed.

Citing Parabellum, Les Sheriff, The Exploited, Ramones, Bad Religion, Suicidal Tendencies and Bérurier noir as influences, underground and alternative, Tagada Jones gradually assimilates various musical currents such as metal, electro, punk rock, hardcore punk or dub.

Tagada Jones builds his universe on realistic texts, sung in French, which advocate respect, freedom or ecology, and criticize globalization, manipulation, wild capitalism, fanaticism or even sexism and intolerance.

Tagada Jones formed in December 1993 in Rennes, Brittany. The band was originally composed of Niko (vocals and guitar), Pascal (guitar), Pepel (bass), and Benoît (drums).
